## 💡 Overview

Tweetify AI is a modern AI-powered tool designed to transform long-form articles into captivating social media posts. Whether you are a content creator, marketer, or developer, Tweetify AI helps you maintain a consistent digital presence without the manual effort.

## 🛠️ Features

- **Instant Repurposing:** Generate social posts from your articles in seconds.
- **Multi-Platform Support:** Tailored content for LinkedIn, Twitter (X), and Instagram.
- **Iterative Refinement:** Chat with the AI to customize and polish your posts until they are perfect.
